code institute

The University credit-rated Online coding bootcamp which has its headquater in Dublin, Ireland is offering to provide interested students a way to get started in achieving their career goals in software development. From the time the post was made on their social media platforms, the institution has recorded an increase in the number of students who are believed to want learn programming but never had the opportunity to take it on themselves.

Code Institute believes in its core goals that there are numerous skillful individuals who lack the know-how to pursue their dream job due to not having the missing piece. The institution holds strongly there is a mismatch between qualifications and workforce requirements, and they are here to deliver the missing middle.

manchester united

Manchester united wins first premier league game with 2-1 against rivals, liverpool after a devastating start to the season.

Jadon Sancho and Marcos Rashford led the reds to regaining their confidence after both players bagged a goal each for the team. Although the ferocious liverpool striker Mohamed salah gave the rivals a consolation goal in the 80th minute, it was not enough for the higly spirited manchester united team to give up their chance to first victory since the 2022/2023 season started.

The relentless manchester united manager, Erik Ten Hag, expresses his delight in the game after the match during a skysports' news interview.

I said to the players, we have to act and not talk a lot. Make sure you are a team and battle and be brave. it's not only about spirit when we are out of possession. Sometimes strikers think they cannot do it because they have to save their energy for offensive actions. Nowadays it's a demand to do both, then you can be a threat in possession and that is what you saw. It makes me happy that Rashford and Sancho make the goals. --Manchester United manager, ETH.

Magnus Carlsen

Magnus carlsen anounces his withdrawal from the next world chess competion on International chess day.

The 30 year Old and a five time chess world champion, known for his attacking style and embarassing grandmasters already at the age of 14, anounces he will not be defending his title as the current chess world champion in the next championship game. According to him i am not very motivated, and i feel like i dont have a lot to gain. This news definitely came as a shock to many of his fans and chess viewers around the world. Now the question is, who will become the next World chess champion?
