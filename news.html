manchester united

Manchester united wins first premier league game after a devastating start to the season.

Jadon Sancho and Marcos Rashford led the reds to regaining their confidence after both players bagged a goal each for the team. Although the ferocious liverpool striker Mohamed salah gave the rivals a consolation goal in the 80th minute, it was not enough for the higly spirited manchester united team to give up their win and hence leading the match to end in 2-1 first win for manchester united since the 2022/2023 season started.

The relentless manchester united manager, Erik Ten Hag, expresses his delight in the game after the match during a skysports news interview.

I said to the players, we have to act and not talk a lot. Make sure you are a team and battle and be brave. it's not only about spirit when we are out of possession. Sometimes strikers think they cannot do it because they have to save their energy for offensive actions. Nowadays it's a demand to do both, then you can be a threat in possession and that is what you saw. It makes me happy that Rashford and Sancho make the goals. --Manchester United manager, ETH.

"Calm down" remixed

Afrobeats superstar, Divine Ikubor popularly known as REMA has finally released the much anticipated remix of his hit single "calm down" which now features American popstar, Selena Gomez. The remix was released on Friday, 26th August 2022 and the video for the pop crossover is coming soon. The song was produced by Andre vibez.

Earlier this week, Selena and Rema both teased the incoming remix on social media with Selena putting out a clip that showed both artists hanging out together. Selena's melodic tune complements Rema's vibe perfectly, maintaining the same energy as the original release.
